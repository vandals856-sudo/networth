Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 10, 2026

Wealthsnap is built on a simple privacy principle: your financial data lives in your Google Sheet, not on our servers. We never see it, store it, or have access to it beyond what's needed to run the app.

1. Who we are

Wealthsnap is an independent personal net worth tracker. For questions about this policy, contact us at vandals856@gmail.com.

2. What data we collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your Google profile photo URL
  • A unique Google user ID

We do not collect your financial data, passwords, bank credentials, or any payment information.

3. How your financial data is stored

This is the most important part of our privacy model.

When you use Wealthsnap, your financial data — account names, balances, net worth history — is stored in a Google Sheet created inside your personal Google Drive. Not on our servers. Not in our database. In your Google Drive.

This means:

  • Only you can access your financial data
  • You can view, edit, export, or delete it at any time directly from Google Sheets
  • Wealthsnap's access is limited to that specific file using Google's OAuth permission system
  • Revoking Wealthsnap's access in your Google Account settings immediately cuts off all access

4. Google OAuth and permissions

Wealthsnap uses Google Sign-In and requests the following permissions:

  • openid, email, profile — to identify you and display your name
  • drive.file — to create and read only the Google Sheet that Wealthsnap itself creates

The drive.file scope is intentionally limited. It does not give us access to the rest of your Google Drive — only files that Wealthsnap creates.

5. How we use your information

We use your Google account information to:

  • Identify your account when you sign in
  • Associate your Google Sheet with your user ID so it can be found on future logins
  • Display your name and profile photo inside the app

We do not sell your information. We do not use your information for advertising. We do not share your information with third parties.

6. Cookies and local storage

Wealthsnap uses your browser's localStorage to store:

  • Your authentication token (so you stay signed in)
  • Your app preferences (theme, profile settings)
  • Cached data to improve performance

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

7. Third-party services

Wealthsnap uses the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth — for authentication
  • Google Sheets API — to read and write your financial data to your Google Drive
  • CoinGecko API — to fetch live cryptocurrency prices (no personal data is sent)

We may in the future use the Anthropic API to generate AI-powered financial insights. When enabled, only your net worth totals and account category summaries are sent — never account names or specific balances.

8. Data retention and deletion

Because your financial data lives in your own Google Sheet, you are in full control of it:

  • To delete your data, simply delete the Google Sheet from your Google Drive
  • To revoke Wealthsnap's access, go to your Google Account → Security → Third-party apps and remove Wealthsnap
  • To clear your local preferences and sign out, use the Sign out option in the app

We do not retain copies of your financial data. Once you delete your Google Sheet and revoke access, no record of your financial information remains.

9. Children's privacy

Wealthsnap is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of Wealthsnap after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

11. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at vandals856@gmail.com.

Wealthsnap is not a financial advisor. This app is for informational purposes only and does not constitute financial, legal, or investment advice.
